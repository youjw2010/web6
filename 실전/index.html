What is F1?

F1 is a form of motorsport that involves racing high-performance cars on various circuits around the world.
It is known for its speed, technology, and the skill of its drivers.

Let's look at f1

1. The car

On the surface, f1 cars are very different from ordinary cars.
This is because they are made with very special designs and appearances to minimize air resistance and speed up.
They feature a sleek body, <>la rge rear wings, and detailed design considering airflow.
